What "Qualified" Actually Means

A qualified lead is a real human with a valid UAE-reachable phone number who matched the budget window of the campaign in their form submission, and is not a duplicate of a recent submission. We filter the rest out on our side. You only see the ones worth calling.

Raw leads are everyone who submitted the form. Qualified leads are the subset we commit to: the ones with a real reachable phone, the right budget, and no recent duplicate. Calling a qualified lead is how you turn the ad spend into a conversation; calling a raw lead is a coin flip.

How Leads Reach You

Every qualified lead is written to a shared Google Sheet the moment it comes in, with the contact details, budget, developer interest, and timestamp. You own the outreach, the pace, and the script. We do not contact your leads ourselves.

What You Own, Not Us

Once a qualified lead lands in your sheet, it is yours. We do not commit to site visits, closing rates, or deal counts. Those outcomes depend on how you work the lead, and we do not want to take credit or blame for work we do not do. We bring the buyers to your door. You turn them into signatures.
